ToastTime Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 23, 2026

Summary

ToastTime does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal data. Everything the app does happens entirely on your device.

Data We Collect

None. ToastTime has no accounts, no analytics, no telemetry, no advertising, and no third-party trackers.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app remembers your last-used speech role and threshold settings using your device's local preferences (UserDefaults on iOS). This data never leaves your device and is removed when you uninstall the app.

Notifications

If you grant notification permission, ToastTime schedules local notifications on your device to fire the bell sounds at your configured thresholds, even if the screen is locked or the app is in the background. These notifications are generated and delivered entirely on your device. No notification content is sent to, or received from, any server.

Network Access

ToastTime does not make any network requests during normal use.

Children's Privacy

ToastTime does not collect information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If the app's data practices ever change, this page will be updated and the new effective date will appear at the top.
